## Big Data
Big [[Data]] refers to extremely large datasets that require advanced tools to process and analyze.

### Characteristics of Big Data:
- **Volume**: Massive amounts of [[data]].
- **Variety**: Structured and unstructured formats.
- **Velocity**: Rapid generation and processing.

### Processing Big Data:
- Requires [[algorithms]] optimized for speed and scalability.
- Often uses distributed systems, such as Hadoop or Spark.

### Ethical Challenges:
- Balancing innovation with privacy concerns.
- Avoiding bias in [[Data]] analysis, which can be mitigated through careful [[data]] cleaning.
